SMA: porting pinctrl & GPIO
===============================================================================
❯ where is gpio drivers for meson/amlogic?

The GPIO for Meson/Amlogic is handled through the pinctrl subsystem (not a standalone GPIO driver):

  drivers/pinctrl/meson/

  Key files:
  - pinctrl-meson.c / pinctrl-meson.h — core driver
  - SoC-specific files:
    - pinctrl-meson8.c, pinctrl-meson8b.c — Meson8/8b
    - pinctrl-meson-gxbb.c, pinctrl-meson-gxl.c — GX series
    - pinctrl-meson-axg.c — AXG
    - pinctrl-meson-g12a.c — G12A
